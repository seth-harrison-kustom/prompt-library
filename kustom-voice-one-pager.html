Kustom Voice — Quick Start

Talk your note. It writes it up and puts it on the job in DASH. About a minute.
PILOT — YOUR OFFICE GOES FIRST

One-time setup (2 min)

  1. On your phone, go to kustom-voice-notes.vercel.app
  2. Sign in with your Kustom Microsoft account (same as email)
  3. Add to home screen:
    iPhone: Safari Share → Add to Home Screen
    Android: Chrome ⋮ → Add to Home screen

⚠️ The one rule

After you tap Stop — WAIT.

Let every word you said finish showing on screen before you continue (the app catches up for a couple seconds). Forgot something? Tap Record more.

Every note — 5 steps

1
Type the job number — just the digits, it adds the dashes. Check the customer + recent notes match your job.
2
Pick the note type — Daily report (prompts you room-by-room) or General note (say anything — a call recap, a decision, an update).
3
Tap ● Record and talk like you're briefing your coordinator — rambling is fine. Say who to loop in: "tag the GM," "make sure Jessica sees this." Watch the mic bar bounce green.
4
Tap Stop, wait for your words, then Continue →
5
Review & Send to DASH — the app shows a clean write-up (fix anything) and who it'll tag (remove any you don't want). Send → it's on the job in DASH with your name on it. Then keep rolling: type the next job right there.

¿Hablas español?

  • Tap Español before recording — prompts, buttons, and your live transcript all switch.
  • Speak Spanish; the office gets a clean English write-up plus your exact Spanish words. Nothing is lost.

Good to know

  • Tagging: say a name or a role ("the coordinator," "the GM"). Can't find someone? The note says so and still posts — tag them by hand.
  • Notes are always private, and the customer is never tagged through this tool.
  • Job not found? Brand-new jobs (today) do that — check the digits, tap continue anyway.
  • Mic bar red? Phone's recording silence — unmute/uncover it and re-record.
