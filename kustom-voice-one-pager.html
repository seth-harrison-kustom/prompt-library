Kustom Voice

Talk for a minute. It writes the note and puts it on the job in DASH.
PILOT — YOU GO FIRST
Why you'll actually want this.
Documenting in DASH is a pain, so it doesn't happen — and then nobody knows what got done. Kustom Voice fixes that: you just talk, on your phone, the way you'd tell a coworker. It cleans up the rambling into a professional note, tags the right people, and drops it on the job — so the work you did today is actually on the record, in seconds, without touching the DASH app.
Two ways to use it — pick one when you record
● Daily Report
Your end-of-day update on a job. The app shows you a checklist of what to cover for that job type — hit those points out loud.
"Hung drywall in the kitchen and living room, painting tomorrow, waiting on the vanity."
● General Note
Any note, any role — a call recap, a decision, an issue, an instruction. Say whatever you need; it writes it up clean. No checklist, no format.
"Talked to the adjuster, they approved the supplement, tell the coordinator to reschedule."

One-time setup (2 min)

  1. Phone → kustom-voice-notes.vercel.app
  2. Sign in with your Kustom Microsoft account
  3. Add to home screen — iPhone: Safari Share → Add to Home Screen · Android: Chrome ⋮ → Add to Home screen

⚠️ The one rule

After you tap Stop — WAIT.

Let every word finish showing on screen before you continue (a couple seconds). Forgot something? Tap Record more.

Recording a note — 5 steps
1
Type the job number — just digits, it adds the dashes. Confirm the customer + recent notes match.
2
Choose Daily Report or General Note (above) — and English or Español.
3
Tap ● Record and talk. Rambling is fine. Say who to loop in — "tag the GM," "make sure Jessica sees this." Mic bar bounces green when it hears you.
4
Tap Stop, wait for your words, tap Continue →
5
Review & Send to DASH. See the clean write-up (fix anything) and who it'll tag (remove any you don't want). Send — it's on the job with your name on it. Next job? Type it right there.

Tagging & privacy

  • Say a name or a role ("the coordinator," "the GM") — it finds them on the job.
  • Can't find someone? The note says so and still posts — tag them by hand.
  • Notes are always private; the customer is never tagged through this tool.

Good to know

  • ¿Hablas español? Tap Español — everything switches; office still gets a clean English write-up plus your exact words.
  • Job not found? Brand-new jobs do that — check the digits, tap continue anyway.
  • Mic bar red? It's recording silence — unmute/uncover the mic, re-record.
