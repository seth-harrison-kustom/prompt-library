Guiding Light

What defines this team.

Revenue, speed, and accuracy matter — but the three things that will define your success here go deeper than the numbers.

CPU Communication

Every note, email, and message you send should answer three things before the reader asks.

CContent & Context — don't be vague. Include all relevant information.
PPurpose — explain why your communication matters and why you're reaching out.
UUrgency — always include timelines. If you don't define it, no one knows when to act.

Curiosity

The defining characteristic of excellent estimators is curiosity — to want to find the full story of every loss, every property, every scope of work.

Estimators are storytellers and revenue drivers. Tell the full story every time. It also pays better.

Accountability

You are responsible for the success and profitability of every job you're assigned to. No "not my job" here.

It's okay to make mistakes. We want $10 mistakes, not $10,000 ones. Own them, fix them, move forward.
