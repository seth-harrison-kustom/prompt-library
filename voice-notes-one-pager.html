Voice Notes — Quick Start

Talk your daily job notes. They land in DASH. Done in about a minute.
PILOT — YOUR OFFICE GOES FIRST

One-time setup (2 min)

  1. On your phone, go to kustom-voice-notes.vercel.app
  2. Sign in with your Kustom Microsoft account (same as email)
  3. Add to home screen:
    iPhone: Safari Share → Add to Home Screen
    Android: Chrome ⋮ → Add to Home screen

⚠️ The one rule

After you tap Stop — WAIT.

Don't continue until every word you said shows on the screen. The app shows a yellow banner to remind you. Forgot something? Tap Record more.

Every note — 5 steps

1
Type the job number (like 26-04-210048) — check the customer name and recent notes match your job
2
Read the "Cover these" list — it's tailored to your job type; hit those topics while you talk
3
Tap ● Record and talk like you're briefing your coordinator — your words appear as you speak and the mic bar bounces green
4
Tap Stop — then WAIT for all your words — then tap Continue →
5
Read the summary, fix anything, tap Send to DASH — your note is on the job: summary first, your exact words below, your name at the bottom. Then "Another note — same job" or "Different job" to keep rolling

¿Hablas español?

  • Tap Español before recording — prompts, buttons, and your live transcript all switch
  • Speak Spanish; the office gets an English summary plus your exact Spanish words — nothing is lost

Good to know

  • Job not found? Brand-new jobs (created today) do that. Check every digit, then tap continue anyway — you'll get an email receipt of your note
  • Mic bar red / "can't hear you"? The phone is recording silence — unmute/uncover the mic and re-record
  • Wrong job pulled up? ← Different job at the top
  • Rambling is fine — the app writes the clean version and you approve it before it sends
  • Top-right shows who's signed in — that name goes on the note
