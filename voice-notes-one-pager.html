Kustom Voice

Talk for a minute. It writes the note and puts it on the job in DASH.
PILOT · YOU GO FIRST
Bring your voice to the team.
You're on the move all day and you know exactly what's happening on your jobs. Kustom Voice is a modern, built-for-the-field way to get it all on the record: you talk, it listens. It turns what you say into a clean, professional DASH note, tags the right people, and puts it on the job for you - in seconds, right from your phone. Your work, captured the moment you finish it.
Two ways to use it - pick one when you record
● Daily Report
Your update on a job. The app shows a short list of what's helpful to cover for that job type - just speak to those points.
"Hung drywall in the kitchen and living room, painting tomorrow, waiting on the vanity."
● General Note
Any note, any role - a call recap, a decision, an update, an instruction. Say whatever you need and it writes it up clean. No list, no format.
"Talked to the adjuster, they approved the supplement, tell the coordinator to reschedule."

One-time setup (1 min)

  1. On your phone, open kustom-voice-notes.vercel.app
  2. Sign in with your Kustom Microsoft account (same as email)
  3. Bookmark it so it's one tap away next time

One quick thing

When you tap Stop, it takes a moment to finish writing out your last words - and Continue lights up the instant it's ready, so you can't cut yourself off. Forgot something? Tap Record more and keep going.

Recording a note - 5 steps
1
Type the job number - just digits, it adds the dashes. Confirm the customer + recent notes match.
2
Choose Daily Report or General Note (above) - and English or Español.
3
Tap ● Record and talk naturally, the way you'd brief a coworker - no need to be formal, it does the cleanup. Say who to loop in: "tag the GM," "make sure Jessica sees this." Mic bar bounces green when it hears you.
4
Tap Stop, then Continue → once it lights up.
5
Review & Send to DASH. See the clean write-up (fix anything) and who it'll tag (remove any you don't want). Send - it's on the job with your name on it. Next job? Type it right there.

Tagging & privacy

  • Say a name or a role ("the coordinator," "the GM") - it finds them on the job.
  • Can't find someone? The note says so and still posts - tag them by hand.
  • Notes are always private; the customer is never tagged through this tool.

Good to know

  • ¿Hablas español? Tap Español - everything switches; office still gets a clean English write-up plus your exact words.
  • Job not found? Brand-new jobs do that - check the digits, tap continue anyway.
  • Mic bar red? It's recording silence - unmute/uncover the mic, re-record.
